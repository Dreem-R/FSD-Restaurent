Story

BINGGE is a web-based food delivery application that came to life during early January 2024. Christine Veigas, Kajal Gupta, Kapil Maheshwari, Nikita Sharma, and Ruchak Khatri, five enthusiastic individuals, started on a journey to revolutionize and change the way people experience food through their innovative web application. It started as a compulsory project, but it quickly turned into a delightful learning experience and an opportunity for exploration. What happened along the way?

These passionate individuals, driven by the desire to simplify food delivery while maintaining their vision of an honest and money-conservative platform, joined together to create a food ordering and delivery platform using their tech-savvy skills. With the guidance of their mentor, Dr. Nismon Rio Robert, they brought their vision to life, launching the first static version of their website in January 2024.
